[h2]Version 1.1 just released, featuring more/better options like auto-disable in arena and allowing pups to revive you! And more! See the full notes in the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/changelog/3632170621]change notes[/url]

[hr][/hr]
[h2]This Mod Supports Two Configurable Methods of Revival:[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]CPR[/b] - Perform chest compressions in a steady rhythm to breathe life back into your dead pals!

[*][b]Proximity[/b] - Stand within a configurable distance of your fallen comrades to guide them back to the land of the living!
[/list]

[hr][/hr]
This mod combines [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2950327774]Revivify by Dual[/url] and [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3396726904]Revivify Meadow by Daimyo[/url] into a singular mod while making the original CPR-based method compatible with [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3388224007]Rain Meadow[/url]. Daimyo's version changed revival to proximity-based, so I took it upon myself to fix the CPR method to work with Meadow while keeping the proximity option as a toggleable alternative.

[hr][/hr]
[h3]Mod Features:[/h3]
[list]
	[*]Fully compatible with Rain Meadow (load [b]after[/b] or [b]above[/b] Rain Meadow for the smoothest experience)

[list]
[*]Other players do [b]not[/b] need the mod installed for [b]you[/b] to be revived in Meadow. They can revive you without it, though they won't see visual feedback. However, you [b]cannot[/b] revive online players without the mod, regardless of how hard you try.
[/list]

	[*]Toggle between proximity-based or CPR-based revival in the Remix menu. With CPR, you must receive compressions to revive. With proximity, someone just needs to stand next to you. CPR also speeds up proximity revival!

    [*]Custom Remix options to adjust revival difficulty, exhaustion mechanics, and more.
[/list]

[hr][/hr]
[h3]How to Perform CPR:[/h3]
[list]
[*]Lay the corpse on a solid surface

[*]Hold the corpse - your slug will assume the CPR position (standing around their neck)

[*]Press grab to compress - stronger compressions revive faster

[*]Time your compressions for maximum effectiveness (compress after you fully finish the animation of the last one)

[*]If you lose rhythm, pause and restart!
[/list]

[hr][/hr]
[h3]Remix Options:[/h3]
They have descriptions now! Hover over them in-game to see their descriptions at the bottom of your screen.

[h3]Known Issues:[/h3]
[list]
	[*]Load order doesn't matter much, but loading after or above Rain Meadow seems smoother

    [*]Corpse piggybacking only works/matters in Singleplayer/Jolly Co-op

    [*]Exhaustion visuals don't sync in Meadow, so other players won't see you become pale

[*]This mod can be used to cheat in public Meadow lobbies - [b]add it to your BannedMods or HighImpactMods lists[/b] if you're worried about others using it to ruin your public lobbies
[/list]
